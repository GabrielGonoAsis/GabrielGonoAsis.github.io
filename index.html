OVERVIEW

Spider-Men Peter Parker and Miles Morales swing back into action in the acclaimed Marvel’s Spider-Man franchise for PS5.

Explore Marvel’s New York with new Web Wings, switching between Peter and Miles to navigate different stories and powers. Confront the menacing Villains as they threatens to destroy their lives and the city they love.

KEY

FEATURES

WEB WINGS

Take Spidey to new heights with Web Wings! Glide through New York city using wind tunnels, mastering the skies with unrivaled speed and freedom.

CHARACTER SWITCHING

Dual the danger, double the heroism! Seamlessly switch between the iconic Peter Parker and the electrifying Miles Morales. Harness their unique powers, styles, and perspectives to face challenges head-on.

SYMBIOTE SUIT

Unleash the darkness within with the Symbiote Suit! Embrace newfound powers as the suit enhances Spidey's abilities to an unprecedented level. Wield incredible strength, agility, and a touch of the sinister.

ICONIC VILLAINS

Duel legendary villains in heart-pounding combat against iconic Spider-Man Villains . The battle for the city's fate reaches new heights as you go head-to-head with your foes in an epic clash of heroism and villainy.

FRIENDS

Ganke Lee

Mile's best friend, confidant, and partner in heroism. He was the second person Miles told about the whole Spider-Man thing and an integral member of the Spider-team.

Hailey Cooper

Community activist, street artist, and one of Mile's closest friends. She's creative, funny, intelligent, and compassionate - especially if you ask Miles.

Harry Osborn

Norman Osborn's son. He has been away for the last few years dealing with the same disease that killed his mom, but he's with a new lease on life.

Norman Osborn

Harr's dad, co-founder of the world's biggest tech company, and disgraced former mayor New York. He's pathological liar but he would do anything to help Harry.

Rio Morales

Some know her as Mile’s cool mom, but most know City Councilwoman Rio Morales for her tireless work to make New York a better and safer place.

MJ Watson

She isn't just reporter for the Bugle. She's also an integral member for the Spider-team, showing that heroes don't need superhuman abilities.

FOES

VENOM

This hulking powerful beast has arrived in New York like a twisted natural disaster. The city has seen a lot over the years, but nothing quite like this.

LIZARD

Pete met 10-foot-tall, out-of-control "Lizard" in college. Nowadays, Dr. Connors has moved on from his past and is Harry Osborn's personal doctor.

SANDMAN

Sandman controls sand to morph his body at will. His ever-shifting form makes him a formidable and elusive foe for the Spider-Men.

KRAVEN

it's unclear yet who is this "Kraven" is. He's clearly here with a purpose. The Spider-Man just need to stop him before they find out what it is.

ABOUT US

We're a passionate game development team based in Burbank, California, and proudly part of PlayStation Studios since 2019. Known for crafting iconic characters, we've swung into the limelight with the critically acclaimed Marvel's Spider-Man series.


From the web-slinging adventures through the iconic streets of Marvel's New York to our earlier creations like Spyro the Dragon and Ratchet and Clank, our commitment to pushing creative boundaries defines us. Join us on this journey where every game is a story, and every story is an adventure. Welcome to the world of Insomniac Games!

ABOUT US

We're a passionate game development team based in Burbank, California, and proudly part of PlayStation Studios since 2019. Known for crafting iconic characters, we've swung into the limelight with the critically acclaimed Marvel's Spider-Man series.

From the web-slinging adventures through the iconic streets of Marvel's New York to our earlier creations like Spyro the Dragon and Ratchet and Clank, our commitment to pushing creative boundaries defines us. Join us on this journey where every game is a story, and every story is an adventure. Welcome to the world of Insomniac Games!

OUR VISION

To create games that have a positive and lasting influence on people’s lives.

Our games promote positive messages. We tell stories about good overcoming evil. We focus on positive themes like friendship, self-sacrifice and fighting for the greater good. And we walk the walk ourselves through our inclusive workplace culture and industry leadership.

OUR MISSIONS

To inspire players with stories of underdogs overcoming colossal challenges

These are the types of stories we love to tell. It’s how we view ourselves too. Even after 25 years, we see ourselves as the scrappy underdog fighting against giants in this industry — and succeeding in the face of what often seem like impossible odds.

To set the industry bar with our craftsmanship.

We take great pride in our work. And we set out to achieve best-in-class results across all of our disciplines, investing heavily in training and development. We consistently work together across departments and our two studio locations. We are open, honest and respectful in all our interactions.
